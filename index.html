About Jalgaon

Jalgaon, a city in Maharashtra, India, is known for its rich agricultural land, especially its famous banana production. It is one of the world's largest banana-producing regions. The city plays a vital role in the agricultural economy of India and is often referred to as the "Banana City."

Banana Production in Jalgaon

Jalgaon is the largest producer of bananas in India and one of the top producers in the world. The region's fertile soil and favorable climate make it an ideal location for banana cultivation. Jalgaon bananas are famous for their quality and are exported worldwide, making them a major contributor to the global banana market.

More than 3 million tonnes of bananas are produced annually in Jalgaon, and it is a significant part of India's agricultural output.
